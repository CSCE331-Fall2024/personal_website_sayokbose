Satisfaction Detector

Engineered a program utilizing OpenCV2 and deepface, leveraging a Convolutional Neural Network to accurately classify 7 distinct human emotions which then determines a customers satisfaction. The program efficiently records and logs unique customer faces’ satisfaction via the devices camera which is then transferred from a database into PowerBI using DAX for intuitive data visualization for business owners helping them to make decisions.
